Walked around Sodermalm today. Nothing particular happened. It was freezing because it snowed this morning but it was my first official day in Stockholm so I wasn't going to sit home. I took about a 2 hour walk around town and did more food shopping.

I found out that there is only one chain that you can buy alcohol and it's owned by the government. I just reminded myself that I have yet to buy any wine for the apartment I am staying at. I will fix that tomorrow.

The first night I went to eat and did some bar hopping with my friend Jakob. By 9pm he could see that my eyes were slowly starting to close at a more rapid rate, so I went home.

I accomplished my first terrifying leap into sleeping in a loft bed. I didn't die yet. But it was only one night. I'm really unclear how this thing is being held together, but I assume they know what they are doing. I'm basically living in an Ikea ad.

I cooked all of my meals today since it was pretty nasty out and since it is pretty expensive. It took only 10 minutes to figure out that I needed a lighter to get the stove working. Better late than never.

I'm learning Swedish words pretty fast due to the fact that all the food I'm buying is written in Swedish. I made this a priority after buying club soda and not water by accident. Live and learn.

The rest of the week looks lovely, so hopefully I can take a picture that's not dark and gloomy.
